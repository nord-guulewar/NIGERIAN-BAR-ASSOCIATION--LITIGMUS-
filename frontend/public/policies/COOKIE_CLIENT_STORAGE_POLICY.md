# Cookie and Client Storage Policy - NBA LITIGMUS

**Effective Date:** June 25, 2026

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy explains how NBA LITIGMUS uses cookies, tokens, browser storage, and similar client-side technologies.

---

## 2. Types of Client-Side Storage

The platform may use:
- Session cookies or equivalent authentication tokens
- Security-related cookies or storage mechanisms
- Functional browser storage for user experience and workflow continuity
- Temporary storage needed for system reliability or offline-capable features, where implemented

---

## 3. Permitted Uses

Client-side storage may be used for:
- Authentication and session continuity
- Security verification and fraud prevention
- User preferences and workflow continuity
- Reliability, recovery, and operational support

NBA LITIGMUS does not use client-side storage for unrelated third-party behavioral advertising.

---

## 4. Security Controls

Where cookies or browser storage are used, they should be configured with appropriate protections such as:
- HttpOnly where applicable
- Secure transport in production
- SameSite restrictions where appropriate
- Limited lifetime and rotation for sensitive tokens

---

## 5. User Choice

Users may be able to control some browser storage behavior through browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies or tokens may prevent use of authentication or core platform functionality.

---

## 6. Changes

If non-essential analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking technologies are introduced, this policy and related disclosures should be updated before deployment.
