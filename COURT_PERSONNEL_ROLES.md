# 🏛️ NBA LITIGMUS - Court Personnel Roles & Structure

## Overview

This document outlines all court personnel roles, their responsibilities, and organizational structure within the NBA LITIGMUS system.

---

## 👥 Court Personnel Roles (16 Total)

### 1. **Administrator** (`admin`)
**Description:** System Administrator with full access

**Responsibilities:**
- System configuration and management
- User account management
- Security settings
- System-wide reports
- Database maintenance

**Required Credentials:**
- None (technical role)

**Access Level:** Full system access

---

### 2. **Court Registrar** (`registrar`)
**Description:** Chief Administrative Officer of the court

**Responsibilities:**
- Overall court administration
- Case file management
- Judge assignment oversight
- Court scheduling
- Staff supervision
- Official court records

**Required Credentials:**
- Legal qualification (LL.B, B.L)
- Bar admission (Supreme Court Number)

**Access Level:** High - Can manage cases, judges, and court operations

---

### 3. **Judge/Justice/Magistrate** (`judge`)
**Description:** Judicial officer presiding over cases

**Responsibilities:**
- Presiding over court proceedings
- Making judicial decisions
- Writing judgments
- Case hearings
- Legal research

**Required Credentials:**
- LL.B (Bachelor of Laws)
- B.L (Barrister at Law)
- Supreme Court Number
- Bar Admission Year

**Access Level:** View assigned cases, update case status, write judgments

---

### 4. **Court Clerk** (`clerk`)
**Description:** Administrative officer handling case documentation

**Responsibilities:**
- Case registration
- File management
- Court records
- Document preparation
- Scheduling assistance

**Required Credentials:**
- Minimum: OND/HND
- Preferred: B.Sc

**Access Level:** Register cases, update case information, view records

---

### 5. **Cashier** (`cashier`)
**Description:** Financial officer handling court payments

**Responsibilities:**
- Receiving payments
- Issuing receipts
- Daily cash reconciliation
- Banking court funds
- Payment records

**Required Credentials:**
- Minimum: OND in Accounting/Finance
- Preferred: HND/B.Sc

**Access Level:** Record payments, issue receipts, view financial records

---

### 6. **Accountant** (`accountant`)
**Description:** Senior financial officer managing court finances

**Responsibilities:**
- Financial management
- Budget preparation
- Financial reports
- Audit compliance
- Payment verification
- Staff salary processing

**Required Credentials:**
- B.Sc in Accounting
- Professional certification (ICAN/ANAN) preferred

**Access Level:** Full financial access, reports, budget management

---

### 7. **Litigation Officer** (`litigation`)
**Description:** Legal officer handling court litigation matters

**Responsibilities:**
- Legal research
- Case preparation
- Legal advice
- Document drafting
- Court representation (in some courts)

**Required Credentials:**
- LL.B (Bachelor of Laws)
- B.L (Barrister at Law)
- Supreme Court Number
- Bar Admission Year

**Access Level:** View cases, legal research, document preparation

---

### 8. **Public Prosecutor** (`prosecutor`)
**Description:** Government legal officer prosecuting criminal cases

**Responsibilities:**
- Criminal prosecution
- Case preparation
- Court representation
- Witness preparation
- Legal opinions

**Required Credentials:**
- LL.B (Bachelor of Laws)
- B.L (Barrister at Law)
- Supreme Court Number
- Bar Admission Year

**Access Level:** View criminal cases, file charges, update prosecution status

---

### 9. **Probate Officer** (`probate`)
**Description:** Officer handling probate and estate matters

**Responsibilities:**
- Probate applications
- Estate administration
- Will verification
- Letters of administration
- Estate records

**Required Credentials:**
- Minimum: HND in Law/Administration
- Preferred: LL.B

**Access Level:** Manage probate cases, estate records

---

### 10. **Records Officer** (`record_officer`)
**Description:** Officer managing court records and archives

**Responsibilities:**
- Records management
- File archiving
- Document retrieval
- Records digitization
- Archive maintenance

**Required Credentials:**
- Minimum: OND in Library Science/Records Management
- Preferred: HND/B.Sc

**Access Level:** View all records, manage archives, document retrieval

---

### 11. **Court Reporter/Stenographer** (`court_reporter`)
**Description:** Officer recording court proceedings

**Responsibilities:**
- Recording court proceedings
- Transcription
- Preparing court transcripts
- Audio/video recording
- Transcript certification

**Required Credentials:**
- Minimum: OND in Secretarial Studies
- Stenography certification
- Typing speed: 60+ WPM

**Access Level:** View cases, record proceedings, prepare transcripts

---

### 12. **Bailiff/Process Server** (`bailiff`)
**Description:** Officer serving court processes and executing orders

**Responsibilities:**
- Serving court summons
- Executing court orders
- Property attachment
- Witness summons
- Service returns

**Required Credentials:**
- Minimum: SSCE
- Preferred: OND

**Access Level:** View assigned cases, update service status

---

### 13. **Court Secretary** (`secretary`)
**Description:** Administrative support officer

**Responsibilities:**
- Administrative support
- Correspondence
- Filing
- Scheduling
- Reception duties

**Required Credentials:**
- Minimum: OND in Secretarial Studies
- Computer literacy

**Access Level:** View cases, administrative functions

---

### 14. **Court Usher** (`usher`)
**Description:** Officer maintaining court decorum

**Responsibilities:**
- Court room management
- Maintaining order
- Announcing cases
- Assisting judges
- Court room setup

**Required Credentials:**
- Minimum: SSCE

**Access Level:** View court schedule, case list

---

### 15. **Security Officer** (`security`)
**Description:** Officer providing court security

**Responsibilities:**
- Court security
- Access control
- Prisoner escort
- Security screening
- Incident reporting

**Required Credentials:**
- Minimum: SSCE
- Security training

**Access Level:** Limited - Security logs, incident reports

---

### 16. **Law Librarian** (`librarian`)
**Description:** Officer managing law library and legal resources

**Responsibilities:**
- Library management
- Legal research assistance
- Book cataloging
- Resource acquisition
- Digital resources

**Required Credentials:**
- B.Sc in Library Science
- Legal knowledge preferred

**Access Level:** Library management, research resources

---

## 🏢 Organizational Structure

### Court Hierarchy

```
Chief Judge/Chief Magistrate
        |
    Registrar
        |
    ----+----+----+----+----+----
    |    |    |    |    |    |
  Judge Clerk Acc. Lit. Prob. Sec.
```

### Departments

1. **Registry Department**
   - Registrar
   - Court Clerks
   - Records Officers
   - Secretary

2. **Judicial Department**
   - Judges
   - Court Reporters
   - Ushers

3. **Legal Department**
   - Litigation Officers
   - Prosecutors
   - Probate Officers

4. **Accounts Department**
   - Accountant
   - Cashiers

5. **Process Department**
   - Bailiffs

6. **Support Services**
   - Security Officers
   - Librarian

---

## 📍 Location Structure

### State Level
- 37 States (36 + FCT)
- Each state has state code (e.g., LA, AB, FC)

### Local Government Level
- **774 LGAs total** across Nigeria
- Each user assigned to specific LGA
- Examples:
  - Lagos: Ikeja, Surulere, Alimosho (20 LGAs)
  - Kano: Kano Municipal, Dala, Gwale (44 LGAs)
  - FCT: Abuja Municipal, Gwagwalada, Kuje (6 Area Councils)

### Court Division
- **Main Court** - Primary court location
- **Magisterial District** - Magistrate court divisions
- **Area Court** - Customary/Area courts
- **Customary Court** - Traditional/customary matters
- **Sharia Court** - Islamic law courts

## 📋 User Account Fields

### Basic Information
- First Name
- Last Name
- Email (unique)
- Phone Number (Nigerian format)
- Password (encrypted)

### Professional Information
- **Role** - One of 16 roles
- **Staff ID** - Unique identifier
- **Date of Employment**
- **Qualification** - SSCE, OND, HND, B.Sc, LL.B, B.L, LL.M, Ph.D

### Location Information
- **State** - 2-letter code (e.g., LA, AB)
- **LGA** - Local Government Area
- **Court Type** - SC, CA, FHC, SHC, SCA, CCA, MC, DC
- **Court Division** - Main, Magisterial District, etc.

### Department Assignment
- Civil
- Criminal
- Family
- Commercial
- Land
- Probate
- Appeal
- Registry
- Accounts
- Records
- Library
- Administration

### Legal Credentials (for legal officers)
- **Bar Admission Year** - Year called to bar
- **Supreme Court Number** - Format: SC/123/2020

## 🚀 API Endpoints

### User Management
```
POST   /api/auth/register          - Register new user
POST   /api/auth/login             - User login
GET    /api/auth/me                - Get current user
PUT    /api/auth/update-profile    - Update profile
```

### Location Data
```
GET    /api/states                 - Get all states
GET    /api/states/:code           - Get state by code
GET    /api/lgas                   - Get all LGAs
GET    /api/lgas/state/:stateCode  - Get LGAs by state
GET    /api/courts                 - Get all court types
```
