# ⚖️ NBA LITIGMUS Logo - Lady Justice Statue

## The Symbol of Justice

The **Lady Justice** statue is the perfect symbol for the NBA LITIGMUS Case Management System. It represents the core values of the Nigerian legal system.

---

## 🎨 Logo Design

### Visual Elements

The logo features a **Lady Justice statue** with three key elements:

```
        👤  ← Head with blindfold
       ⚖️⚔️  ← Scales (left) & Sword (right)
       |||  ← Flowing robe
       ===  ← Base/platform
```

### Color Scheme

| Element | Color | Meaning |
|---------|-------|---------|
| **Body & Robe** | NBA Green (#1a472a) | Authority, Stability, Justice |
| **Scales** | Gold (#d4af37) | Fairness, Balance, Impartiality |
| **Sword** | Gold (#d4af37) | Power, Authority, Enforcement |

## 📖 Symbolism

### 1. The Blindfold 👁️
**Meaning:** Impartiality and Objectivity

The blindfold represents that justice should be:
- **Blind to bias** - No favoritism
- **Blind to status** - Equal treatment for all
- **Blind to wealth** - Rich and poor treated equally
- **Blind to power** - No one is above the law

In the Nigerian context, this emphasizes that the law applies equally to all citizens, regardless of their background, ethnicity, religion, or social status.

### 2. The Scales ⚖️
**Meaning:** Balance and Fairness

The scales (in gold) represent:
- **Weighing evidence** - Careful consideration
- **Balance** - Both sides heard equally
- **Fairness** - Just decisions
- **Equilibrium** - Harmony in justice

The scales remind us that every case in LITIGMUS must be:
- Thoroughly examined
- Fairly judged
- Properly balanced
- Justly decided

### 3. The Sword ⚔️
**Meaning:** Authority and Enforcement

The sword (in gold) symbolizes:
- **Power of law** - Authority to enforce
- **Swift justice** - Timely decisions
- **Protection** - Defending the innocent
- **Punishment** - Consequences for wrongdoing

In LITIGMUS, this represents:
- The court's authority to make binding decisions
- The power to enforce judgments
- Protection of citizens' rights
- Upholding the rule of law

### 4. The Robe 👗
**Meaning:** Dignity and Formality

The flowing robe represents:
- **Dignity** - Respect for the legal process
- **Formality** - Proper procedures
- **Tradition** - Heritage of justice
- **Authority** - Official capacity

### 5. The Platform/Base 🏛️
**Meaning:** Foundation and Stability

The solid base represents:
- **Strong foundation** - Built on solid principles
- **Stability** - Consistent application of law
- **Permanence** - Enduring justice system
- **Support** - Upholding society

- Effect: Gentle breathing motion
